Design System Inspiration

Design System Inspiration of Letters

A calm, clinical visual system for an AI letter-and-transcription tool built for clinicians. Pale sky-blue panels bookend every page, body sections sit on quiet near-white surfaces, and a single signature accent of sky-blue carries every hint of brand color. Open Runde — a friendly rounded sans — sets every UI string; a black primary pill holds the rare moment of high contrast in an otherwise airy, low-saturation interface.

01 — Colors

Touch Targets

  • All buttons sit at 44–48px height — comfortably above WCAG AAA's 44×44 minimum.
  • Source chips inside the demo card are 40×40 at desktop and expand to fit a full-width chip row on mobile.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Nav reduces to small Letters wordmark + a single primary Sign-up pill on mobile; secondary links move into a sheet.
  • Feature row collapses 3-up → 1-up; cards retain 24px corners and 24px inner padding.
  • Specialty grid drops 4-up → 2-up at tablet, then 1-up on mobile, but tiles retain fixed height so the wall reads as a stack.
  • Pricing stacks vertically with the featured Pro tier in the middle position; the embedded black CTA remains the visual anchor.
  • Hero gradient panel narrows its inner padding but preserves the 48px corners and the gradient sweep — the bookend feel survives every viewport.
